Disentangled Representation Learning for Controllable Generation (DRL for Real)

What is DRL?

Disentangled Representation Learning (DRL) enables AI systems to understand and separate the fundamental factors of variation in data. This approach:

  • Enhances AI's ability to understand the world
  • Reduces hallucination in language and multimodal models
  • Improves controllability in generative systems
  • Strengthens model robustness and interpretability

Current Challenges

Despite its potential, DRL faces a critical limitation:

  • Lack of realistic benchmarks and unified evaluation metrics
  • Limited application beyond synthetic scenarios
  • Gap between theoretical research and practical implementation

Workshop Focus

The "DRL for Real" workshop bridges this gap by:

  • Introducing novel, realistic datasets
  • Developing comprehensive benchmarks for practical evaluation
  • Exploring applications in controllable generation and autonomous driving
