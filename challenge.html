2025 Disentangled Representation Learning Challenge

ICCV 2025 DRL for Real Workshop

02 Challenge

Topic 1: Real-world Benchmarks for Disentangled Representation Learning

  • Participants need to develop disentangled representation learning methods that perform well on real-world data, which will be evaluated through new comprehensive benchmarks.
  • Evaluation will focus on model robustness, interpretability, and generalization capabilities, especially when handling complex, variable real-world data.

Topic 2: Applications of Disentangled Representation Learning in Controllable Generation

  • Participants need to develop highly controllable generative models utilizing disentangled representation learning, capable of precisely manipulating specific attributes of generated content.
  • Evaluation will focus on generation quality, control precision, degree of attribute disentanglement, and model generalization capabilities on unseen attribute combinations.
  • Winners of each topic will receive prize money and certificates, with specific prize amounts to be announced at the start of the challenge.
  • Winning teams will be invited to present their methods at the ICCV 2025 DRL for Real Workshop and will have the opportunity to publish their work in a special issue.
  • All participants will receive participation certificates, recognizing their contributions to disentangled representation learning research.

Challenge Schedule

  • Challenge Launch and Dataset Release: January 2025
  • Preliminary Submission Deadline: April 2025
  • Final Submission Deadline: June 2025
  • Winner Notification: July 2025
  • Technical Report Deadline (Winners): August 2025
